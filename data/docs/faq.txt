Q: How do I request time off?
A: Submit a request via the HR portal at hr.conversy.internal at least 2 weeks in advance.

Q: How do I expense a purchase?
A: Use the Expense app, attach your receipt, and submit to your manager for approval within 30 days.

Q: What is the remote work policy?
A: Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Q: How do I onboard a new team member?
A: Contact HR for the onboarding checklist. IT access is set up within 24 hours of start date.

Q: What health benefits are available?
A: All full-time employees receive Friskvårdsbidrag (wellness allowance) of 5,000 SEK per year.
